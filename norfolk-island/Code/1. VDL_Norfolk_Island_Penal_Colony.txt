VDL Norfolk Island Penal Colony

A reconstructed register of convicts detained at the Norfolk Island Penal Colony. Data was prepared by Dr Tim Causer (University College London) and curated for the Prosecution Project by Mark Finnane and Lisa Durnian. 

This dataset contains records from the following registers

Register Name       : VDL_NIPC
Jurisdiction        : NSW 1825–44, VDL 1844–55
Register Source     : Data has been prepared from original archival sources by Tim Causer
Register Identifier : not applicable
Description         : Data (including penal transportation, shipping, convict administration and court records) has been prepared from original archival sources by Tim Causer, in the course of his doctoral research at the Menzies Centre for Australian Studies, King’s College London. The research was funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council’s Doctoral Awards scheme.
In interpreting the data, users are advised to consult the ‘list of abbreviations’ as well as the list of sources, both included in metadata attached to the data file.
